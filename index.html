About

Our mission at Music Data Studio is to expertly manage public and private data, along with data crafted from our industry expertise and AI, to establish a comprehensive and innovative database for the live music industry.

At Music Data Studio, our specialty lies in the comprehensive collection of data. We meticulously track over hundreds of thousands of artists, continuously updating their profiles each day using our advanced algorithmic technology. This process involves connecting multiple databases, creating an integrated and rich data ecosystem. Further enhancing our capabilities, we apply our industry expertise in conjunction with artificial intelligence, refining our data to offer unparalleled insights.

We have developed a high-performance API designed to seamlessly integrate our data across a variety of live music industry solutions. Our goal is to maintain the most reliable database to predict the trends and shifts in the live music sector both today and in the future, grounded in our deep understanding of the industry.

Tools using the Music Data Studio database

Launched in 2021, Bookr.fm, developed by us, has become the go-to solution for festival booking, offering a comprehensive service from artist discovery to optimizing artistic choices. Today, it is used by several festivals across Europe.
